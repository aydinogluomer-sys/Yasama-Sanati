# Responsive Rules

## Desktop

- Use wider editorial breathing room.
- Keep hierarchy strong and spread out.
- Preserve premium rhythm, not just coverage.

## Tablet

- Re-stack with intention.
- Keep content readable and balanced.
- Reduce decorative density if it crowds the layout.

## Mobile

- Prioritize clarity over decoration.
- Keep CTA paths visible.
- Keep form fields touch-friendly.
- Hide purely decorative layers that compete with reading.

## Rules

- Mobile should be a deliberate design, not a compressed desktop.
- Never allow important contact information to wrap awkwardly.
- Avoid horizontal overflow at every size.
