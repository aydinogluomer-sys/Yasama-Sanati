# Section Rhythm

## Page Rhythm

The page should feel like a sequence of authored scenes rather than independent blocks.

## Rhythm Pattern

1. Promise
2. Clarify
3. Prove
4. Deepen
5. Convert
6. Close

## Spacing Intent

- The first section should establish confidence quickly.
- Middle sections should expand trust without losing clarity.
- The final section should feel like a composed ending.

## Rules

- Repetition must be intentional.
- Transitions between sections should feel smooth, not abrupt.
- Do not allow every section to look visually equivalent.

visible rhythm break in a scroll recording. Heading scale also varies (kickers + serif leads)
so no two adjacent sections read as the same template block.
