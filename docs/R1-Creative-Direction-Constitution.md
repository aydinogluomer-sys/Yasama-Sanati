# R1 — Creative Direction Constitution

**Status:** ACTIVE
**Scope:** Awwwards 90+ candidate redesign phase
**Authority level:** Binding creative authority for the new redesign phase
**Supersedes (creatively):** the 5% Diff / "impossible to name" refinement regime
**Does not supersede:** Golden Master rollback baseline, accessibility, reduced-motion parity, production stability, Emotional Journey contract, Innovation protection, core brand DNA

---

## 1. Purpose and Authority

### Purpose

R1 is the creative constitution for the new **Awwwards 90+ candidate redesign phase** of Yaşama Sanatı. It exists to give the redesign a single, short, operational source of creative truth — so that visible change is authored deliberately rather than drifting.

This document is intentionally concise. It is not a master plan, not a 25–40 page bible, and not a 30-phase roadmap. It defines *direction, boundaries, and acceptance* — not implementation.

### Authority

R1 is the creative authority for the redesign phase. Where creative judgment is required, R1 governs. It does not authorize implementation by itself: visible work begins only after the relevant Wave's visual proof is approved (see §10).

### Authority Update — historical vs. active

The previous phase operated under a **refinement constitution** whose core premise was that the current build was the creative source of truth and could only be nudged. For the new redesign phase, the following rules are now **historical records only** — they describe how the refinement phase worked, and they remain valid as history, but they no longer govern:

- *current build as creative source of truth*
- *preserve 90% as a hard requirement*
- *changes must be impossible to name*
- *5% Diff as the only implementation unit*
- *BlindPremiumAcceptance as final creative acceptance*

The following remain **active and binding** in the redesign phase:

- **Golden Master** as the technical rollback baseline (rollback reference, not a creative target)
- **Accessibility** requirements
- **Reduced-motion parity**
- **Production stability** requirements
- The **Emotional Journey six-stage contract**
- **Innovation protection**
- Core **Yaşama Sanatı brand DNA**

### Current build status

**The current build is the technical starting state, not the new visual target.** It may be used as the technical baseline and rollback reference (via Golden Master), but it is **not** the binding creative target for the new R1 redesign phase. History is not rewritten and prior acceptance documents are not deleted; the current build simply stops being the creative goal — it is the floor the redesign starts from, not the ceiling it aims for.

### On prior governance documents

Previous governance and acceptance documents (the refinement constitution, BlindPremiumAcceptance, the D-series diff records, the Golden Master capture notes) are **preserved as historical records**. They are not edited, deleted, or rewritten. History stands. From this point forward, **R1 governs the new Awwwards candidate redesign direction** while those documents remain the record of the phase that produced the current build.

## 2. Selected Direction

The binding creative direction is fixed. It is **not** reopened in this phase.

**PRIMARY — Route A: Editorial Wellness Academy**
The site reads as a serious, calm, contemporary academy with editorial authorship. Typographic hierarchy, considered composition, and human warmth are the primary quality levers.

**SECONDARY ACCENT — Route C: Restrained Meridian Intelligence**
A quiet undercurrent of breath / meridian intelligence acts as a *connecting accent* between sections and moments. It is restraint, not spectacle. It threads the experience together; it never headlines it.

**Route B — bounded influence only.**
Route B may influence **only**:

- lighting
- image crop
- pacing
- selected transition atmosphere

Route B **must not become the primary identity.** It is atmosphere, not authorship.

> **Decision is closed.** Creative direction selection is not reopened in R1.

## 3. Brand Character

Yaşama Sanatı must feel:

- academic
- calm
- human
- contemporary
- editorial
- deeply considered
- premium **without** luxury-hotel language
- ritualistic **without** mysticism
- cinematic **without** becoming a film gimmick
- spiritual **without** cosmic spectacle
- intelligent **without** becoming a technology demo

It must **not** resemble:

- a generic wellness template
- a yoga retreat
- a luxury resort
- a fashion portfolio
- a SaaS product
- an agency showcase
- a spiritual technology demo
- an AI-generated mystical wellness site

These two lists are the standing taste test. Any proposed direction that pulls the site toward the "must not" column is wrong regardless of how polished it is.

## 4. Emotional Journey

The official Emotional Journey is a **six-stage contract**. These stages are fixed. They are not added to, renamed, reordered, or replaced.

1. **Karşılanma**
2. **Merak**
3. **Keşif**
4. **Yakınlaşma**
5. **Güven**
6. **Karar**

### Official section mapping

| Section | Stage |
|---|---|
| Hero | Karşılanma |
| Introduction | Karşılanma / Merak |
| WellnessSanctuary | Merak / Keşif |
| Innovation | Keşif **peak** |
| SignatureTypeScene | Keşif / brand memory |
| ElementisStory | Yakınlaşma |
| SustainableRetreat | Güven |
| Form | Karar |
| Footer | Karar / soft closure |

### Not official stages

Words like **Hayranlık, Yenilenme, Huzur, Dönüşüm** may describe a *local* feeling within a section, but they are **not** official journey stages and must not be introduced as such. The contract has six stages — no more.

## 5. Preserve / Replace Matrix

This matrix translates the direction into what holds and what may change. "Preserve" means protected identity; "Evolve" means authored visible change is authorized within budget; "Replace" means the current composition may be reconceived (not merely nudged) within budget.

| Area | Disposition | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Innovation (structure, interaction, cards, storytelling, internal visual language) | **Preserve (hard)** | Experiential peak. Transition boundary only. |
| SignatureTypeScene brand-memory role | **Preserve (hard)** | The typographic brand-memory role remains protected; Innovation remains the Keşif peak. |
| Footer emotional closure | **Preserve (role)** | Closure role fixed; transition/closure refinement allowed. |
| Core olive / cream / copper identity | **Preserve (hard)** | Palette identity is non-negotiable. |
| Accessibility / reduced-motion parity / production stability | **Preserve (hard)** | Always-on constraints. |
| Emotional Journey six stages + mapping | **Preserve (hard)** | Contract, not a suggestion. |
| Hero | **Evolve** | Authored opening; not a familiar wellness poster. |
| Introduction | **Evolve** | Resolve empty space; strengthen the Hero→Introduction bridge. |
| WellnessSanctuary | **Replace composition** | Break the predictable split layout. |
| ElementisStory | **Evolve** | Strengthen the human / Yakınlaşma connection. |
| SustainableRetreat (mobile) | **Replace composition (mobile)** | Mobile composed independently, not squeezed. |
| Form | **Replace** | From utility form to decision scene. |
| Section transitions | **Evolve** | At least three intentional, memorable transitions. |

Golden Master is the **rollback reference** for all of the above — a safety net, **not** a creative target to return to.

## 6. Protected Areas

**Hard protected — do not redesign:**

- Innovation structure
- Innovation interaction concept
- Innovation card system
- Innovation storytelling
- Innovation internal visual language
- SignatureTypeScene's brand-memory role
- Footer's emotional closure
- core olive / cream / copper identity
- accessibility
- reduced-motion parity
- production stability

**Golden Master** is a rollback reference, not a creative target.

The redesign moves *around* these protected cores. Innovation in particular is the experiential peak and must emerge from the redesign **at least as strong as before** — never weakened to make a neighboring section shine.

## 7. Authorized Change Budget

Visible redesign is **explicitly authorized** for:

- Hero
- Introduction
- WellnessSanctuary
- SustainableRetreat mobile composition
- Form
- selected section transitions

### Indicative change budgets

These are **creative budgets** — an expression of how much visible change is sanctioned per area. They are not implementation instructions and not pixel quotas.

| Section | Budget | Intent |
|---|---|---|
| Hero | 20–30% | Authored opening within existing calm. |
| Introduction | 20–30% | Resolve empty space; bridge from Hero. |
| WellnessSanctuary | 35–45% | Reconceive the split-layout predictability. |
| Innovation | 0–5% | **Transition boundary only.** Core untouched. |
| SignatureTypeScene | 0–10% | Preserve brand-memory peak; refine only. |
| ElementisStory | 10–20% | Strengthen human connection. |
| SustainableRetreat | 25–35% | Especially mobile composition. |
| Form | 40–50% | Utility → decision scene. |
| Footer | 10–15% | Transition / closure only. |

Where a section is hard-protected (Innovation, SignatureTypeScene role), the budget is a ceiling on *peripheral* change, never a license to touch the protected core.

## 8. Global Silhouette Principles

The redesign should:

- preserve Yaşama Sanatı's existing **seriousness and calm**
- increase **authorship and memorability**
- improve **page-level dramaturgy**
- make section transitions more **intentional**
- strengthen **mobile-specific composition**
- avoid generic wellness stock language
- avoid luxury retreat drift
- avoid mystical-tech drift
- avoid agency portfolio drift
- use **editorial hierarchy** as the main quality lever
- use **meridian / breath intelligence** only as a restrained connecting accent

The silhouette of the page — its large-scale rhythm of mass, void, and pacing — is the primary instrument. Editorial hierarchy leads; atmosphere (Route B) and meridian accent (Route C) support. The page is composed, not decorated.

---

## 9. Three Signature Moments

There are **exactly three** primary signature moments. No more are added.

**1. Opening Breath**
Hero + the Hero-to-Introduction transition. The first breath of the site: an authored arrival that sets calm, seriousness, and curiosity in motion.

**2. Protected Innovation Core**
The existing Innovation experience remains the experiential **peak**. It is not reinvented; it is protected and arrived at well.

**3. Decision Ritual**
The Form-to-Footer transition transforms conversion into a **calm personal decision** rather than a utility submission.

Secondary refinements (smaller authored transitions, considered micro-moments) are allowed, but **the page must not become an effects showcase.** Three primary signature moments must remain individually legible.

## 10. Wave Plan

The redesign proceeds in four waves. **Visual proof precedes implementation.** Implementation does not begin before the relevant Wave's proof is approved.

### Wave 1 — Opening Proof

**Scope:** Hero · Hero-to-Introduction bridge · Introduction
**Requirement:** Desktop **and** mobile visual proof must be approved **before any implementation.**
**Purpose:** Prove the new direction on the opening before the rest of the site changes.

### Wave 2 — Discovery and Trust

**Scope:** WellnessSanctuary · Innovation entry/exit transition only · SustainableRetreat mobile strategy
**Requirement:** **Innovation itself remains protected** — transition boundary work only.

### Wave 3 — Human and Decision

**Scope:** ElementisStory connection · Form redesign · Footer closure
**Purpose:** Turn the end of the page from utility conversion into a calm personal decision.

### Wave 4 — System Coherence

**Scope:** transition consistency · mobile-specific composition · typography consistency · reduced-motion equivalent · final browser QA
**Purpose:** Resolve the redesign into one coherent system and verify parity and stability.

> Wave 1 is the gate. The direction earns the rest of the site by proving the opening first.

## 11. Creative Acceptance Criteria

The redesign **cannot be accepted** unless **all** of the following hold:

- the before/after difference is **visibly clear**
- Hero feels **authored**, not like a familiar wellness poster
- Introduction no longer contains **unresolved empty space**
- WellnessSanctuary no longer reads as a **predictable split layout**
- Innovation remains intact and **at least as strong** as before
- SignatureTypeScene remains the **brand-memory typographic peak**
- Form becomes a **decision scene**, not a utility form
- Footer **closes the decision calmly**
- mobile layouts are **independently composed**, not desktop squeezed down
- at least **three transitions** are intentional and memorable
- **exactly three** primary signature moments remain legible
- **no luxury-hotel drift** occurs
- **no mystical-tech drift** occurs
- **no generic AI wellness drift** occurs
- **no SaaS / agency portfolio drift** occurs
- **accessibility and reduced-motion parity remain intact**
- final acceptance imagery contains **no development, error, or issue badges**

Acceptance is all-or-nothing on this list. A polished section that fails one criterion has not passed.

---

## 12. Stop Conditions

Stop the redesign direction if **any** of the following occur:

- Innovation is **weakened**
- the site starts resembling a **luxury resort**
- the site starts resembling a **generic yoga / wellness template**
- the site starts resembling a **tech demo**
- the site becomes **too cinematic** and loses academic calm
- mobile typography becomes **fragile**
- **readability is sacrificed** for art direction
- **reduced-motion users receive an inferior experience**
- implementation tries to **skip visual proof**
- implementation **begins before Wave 1 proof approval**

A stop condition is a hard halt, not a warning. When one is hit, the direction pauses for correction before any further visible change.

---

## 13. Decision

R1 is the active creative constitution for the Awwwards 90+ candidate redesign phase. The selected direction is **Route A — Editorial Wellness Academy** as primary, with **Route C — Restrained Meridian Intelligence** as a restrained connecting accent, and Route B bounded to lighting, image crop, pacing, and selected transition atmosphere only. Previous acceptance and refinement documents remain historical records; R1 governs the new direction. The six-stage Emotional Journey, Innovation, SignatureTypeScene's brand-memory role, the core olive/cream/copper identity, accessibility, reduced-motion parity, and production stability remain protected and binding. Visible change is authorized within the §7 budgets, proven Wave by Wave, and accepted only against §11.

R1 ACTIVE — Editorial Wellness Academy with Restrained Meridian Intelligence
