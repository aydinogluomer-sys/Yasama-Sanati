# Art Direction

## Direction Selected

Editorial Wellness Academy

## Why This Direction

Yaşama Sanatı needs to feel like a small creative studio built a premium learning and healing platform, not like a generic spiritual template. The site should be calm, precise, and authored.

## Audience Segments

1. Breathwork and regulation seeker
2. Reiki / meridian therapy interested user
3. Hypnotherapy or transformation learner
4. Trust-driven education buyer

## Core Promise

The site should communicate that body, mind, and energy are treated as one coherent system through education, sessions, and embodied practice.

## Narrative Keywords

- breath
- ritual
- stillness
- clarity
- embodied intelligence
- warm precision
- trust
- craft

## What the Site Is

- A premium editorial wellness academy
- A trustworthy learning and healing destination
- A refined brand with measured motion and strong hierarchy

## What the Site Is Not

- A generic spiritual landing page
- A noisy SaaS template with wellness copy pasted over it
- A decorative page with many effects and weak content structure

## Visual Implications

- Strong typography hierarchy
- Large negative space
- Calm color system
- Controlled contrast
- Sparse, meaningful motion
- Strong editorial closing scene in the footer

## Section-Level Intent

- Hero: instant understanding
- Introduction: clear definition
- Wellness / approach: method and tone
- Story: trust and origin
- Retreat / innovation: breadth without clutter
- Form: conversion with calm confidence
- Footer: final authored closing

## Guardrails

- Do not add visual noise to compensate for weak narrative.
- Do not mix multiple aesthetic systems in one page.
- Do not chase “Awwwards” with effects instead of structure.

# Round 2 — Silhouette Reset (this sprint)

## What changed in the diagnosis

Round 1 chose the *right* direction (Editorial Wellness Academy) but executed it too
cautiously. The round-1 typography guardrail — "headings deliberate, **not oversized by
default**" — is precisely what kept the site looking like a well-built clone. The serif was
loaded and never used; nothing reads editorial.

Round 2 keeps the direction but **commits to scale and authored composition** that round 1
only documented on paper. Restraint moves from *scale* → *motion*: type can be dramatic and
large; motion stays purposeful and never noisy.

## Three creative routes (scored 0–10)

| Route | Brand fit | Awwwards potential | Impl. risk | Perf risk | Conversion clarity | Verdict |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---|
| **A — Editorial Wellness Academy** (Aesop/Kinfolk; refined serif+sans rhythm, large negative space, oversized serif display) | 9.5 | 8.5 | Low | Low | High | **PRIMARY** |
| B — Cinematic Healing Studio (dark olive, huge image masses, slow transitions) | 8.0 | 8.8 | Med | Med (large media) | Med | Borrow pacing only |
| **C — Meridian Intelligence** (energy-line / anatomy / breath metaphor, scroll-drawn meridian) | 7.5 | 9.2 | Med | Med (3D/SVG) | Med | **ACCENT layer** |

*Impl./Perf risk: lower is better.*

## Decision

- **Primary route: A — Editorial Wellness Academy.**
- **Signature accent: C — Meridian Intelligence**, expressed as a scroll-drawn meridian/breath
  line (Phase 9), not a full tech-demo. Keeps the site human and editorial while giving it one
  unmistakable, brand-specific motif.
- Route B contributes only its *pacing* (slow, deliberate transitions) — not full-bleed dark
  cinema, which would hurt conversion clarity.

## Revised typography stance (overrides round-1 caution)

- Serif (Cormorant Garamond) becomes the **display voice** for hero + major section statements.
- A clamp-based display scale is authorized up to ~12vw (hero) — big, breathing, editorial.
- Grotesque (Basis) stays for body, labels, UI. Two voices, clear contrast.
- Manual line breaks are phased out in favor of responsive line/word reveal.

## Mood per zone (Round 2)

- **Hero:** asymmetric editorial opening — oversized serif headline broken into deliberate
  lines, a small handwritten human mark, calm video/visual mass, a thin meridian line. One
  signature opening moment in the first ~1.2s.
- **Introduction:** image-led editorial block; lead statement set in large serif; responsive
  line reveal (no hardcoded breaks).
- **Programs / discovery:** scroll-linked card stage with fluid crossfade between states.
- **Story / method:** narrow text column + generous empty field + one handwritten note.
- **Form / CTA:** calm high-contrast closure; one premium primary action.
- **Footer:** keep round-1 strength as the authored closing scene.

## What Round 2 will NOT do

- Will not reorder sections.
- Will not turn the site into a dark cinematic tech demo (Route B/C overreach).
- Will not add decorative glow, particles, or generic "AI wellness" effects.
- Will not regress accessibility or performance to buy spectacle.
