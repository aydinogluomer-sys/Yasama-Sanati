# UX Flow

## Main Goal

Help the visitor understand what Yaşama Sanatı offers, why it is trustworthy, and what action to take next.

## Current Home Flow

1. Hero
2. Introduction
3. Wellness / approach
4. Innovation
5. Story
6. Sustainable retreat / broader narrative
7. Contact form
8. Footer
9. Navigation overlay

## Flow Assessment

### Hero

- Strength: strong opening attitude and strong visual presence.
- Risk: the promise must stay readable instantly.

### Introduction

- Strength: explains the academy clearly.
- Risk: too much text could flatten the first transition.

### Wellness / approach

- Strength: good place to explain method and tone.
- Risk: needs a tighter relationship to conversion.

### Innovation

- Strength: can support credibility and breadth.
- Risk: can become a separate brand story if not anchored.

### Story

- Strength: builds trust and origin.
- Risk: must not repeat the introduction.

### Retreat

- Strength: broadens the story into experience.
- Risk: needs clear relevance to the primary service flow.

### Contact form

- Strength: gives a direct action.
- Risk: must feel guided and not generic.

### Footer

- Strength: closes the page with confidence.
- Risk: if too decorative, it can pull away from conversion.

## Primary CTA Sequence

1. Understand the offer.
2. Build trust.
3. Explore programs.
4. Read the story or method.
5. Contact / request a session.

## Mobile Flow Rules

- Keep the opening promise short.
- Keep CTA options easy to reach.
- Avoid compressed side-by-side layouts that read as desktop reduced.
- Ensure the form remains readable and touch-friendly.
- Keep the footer calm and legible.

## Flow Problems To Watch

- Repeated messaging across Introduction, Story, and Wellness sections.
- Decorative density that slows comprehension.
- Weak handoff from narrative content to contact action.
- Footer becoming visually stronger than the content above it.

## Flow Target

The page should feel like a guided editorial journey:

Promise -> Clarify -> Prove -> Deepen -> Convert -> Close
